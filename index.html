Where do you get all the cute pictures? I use Pixiv, which is the Japanese equivalent of DeviantArt. I find much more art I enjoy there. If you want to find as many cute images as I have, I suggest searching in Japanese (i.e. using Japanese characters instead of English). By suggestion of cel, you can see all the pictures I used as well as the original source (or as close as possible)!



What font do you use? For the navigation pane and small banners, I use Sniglet. For the larger section banners (300x130), I'd rather keep it to myself.

How are you so good at coding? It's not so much as being good as understanding the code and having patience in case something doesn't turn out the way it should. I admit that I don't remember every single code. What's important is being resourceful and search for ways to make your ideas come to life (through code, of course). Be systematic and plan out what you want to do first!

Isn't your name a little long? Yes, but that was when I was younger and naive to the evil powers that lie in character limits! I don't use drumblastingquilava anymore. On August 4, 2015, I changed my name to quilava without paying for it! Success!

No borders? Nope. I think they look really tacky. Plus, it would interfere with the background, which looks so relaxing as it is.

Can you send me your code? Yes, of course! I can explain things to the best of my knowledge, but most of the fun comes from playing around with the code yourself!

Do you play any other sites similar to CC? No, I don't. This is my home!

So, how much money have you spent on the site? Nothing! Yet... maybe in a few years, when I'm sure that I won't leave the site. But things are looking towards me staying on the site being a part of this great community!
